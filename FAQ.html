CYPRESS

City of Toronto

FAQ

What is CYPRESS

Cypress is a system that allows citizens to report issues they encounter in their city. It will allow them to follow up on the process of their problem from the day it’s reported until it is resolved.

How to report a problem?

In order to make a report you must register for an account and then go into the Submit Report tab and enter the details of your problem.

How long will it take for the City of Toronto to review the claim?

All reports are automatically processed through the system and are reviewed by the Toronto City Council as soon as possible. The app will continuously keep the user updated through a series of notifications until the reported problem has been resolved.

What should I do if I made an error when reporting a problem?

If you have made a mistake while making a report just go into the View Reports tab and click the edit button next to your report.

Will I be anonymous when reporting a problem?

This site will collect and maintain personal information provided by its users, including their names, addresses, mobile telephone numbers, email addresses, and so forth. The information provided will be used and will only be accessible by the City of Toronto. It will not be used in a predatory way.
